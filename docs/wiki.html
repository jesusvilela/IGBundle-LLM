2.2 Ablation Study

Removing geometric components systematically degrades performance, proving their necessity.

  • No Curvature Loss: -9.5% accuracy drop.
  • No Natural Gradients: -8.4% accuracy drop.
  • Euclidean Geometry (κ=0): -10.9% accuracy drop.
